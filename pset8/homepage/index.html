Alonso

Who am i? There are times in which i ask myself that same question over and over again. Who am i if not a volcano in constant explosion? if not a myriad of combined yet disaggregated colours? Who am i if not a sea filled with fearless creatures and unknown pleasures to the human eye? i have been a wanderer in this vast ocean that is my existence for an immesurable amount of time. And throughout that time, there is one thing i know for sure about myself; and that is: i will not know anything until i take my last breath.
